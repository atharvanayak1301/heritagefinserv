About Loans in India

Understand loan products and smart borrowing

What is a Loan?

A loan is a financial facility provided by a bank or NBFC where a borrower receives a certain amount and agrees to repay it with interest over a specified period. Loans help individuals and businesses meet large financial needs without immediate full payment.

Why Take a Loan?

  • Fulfill personal or business goals
  • Manage large expenses easily
  • Flexible repayment options
  • Build credit history

Factors Affecting Loan Approval

  • Income and employment stability
  • Credit score
  • Loan amount and tenure
  • Existing liabilities

Smart Borrowing Tips

Always borrow according to your repayment capacity. Compare interest rates, check processing fees, and choose a tenure that balances EMI and total interest cost.
